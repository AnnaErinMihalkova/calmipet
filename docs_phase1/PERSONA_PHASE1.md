# Primary User Persona — Phase 1

## Maya (17) — Busy High School Junior
- Context: Balances classes, extracurriculars, and exam prep; often studies late.
- Goals: Notice stress early, take quick reset breaks, feel more control daily.
- Pain Points: Doesn’t realize stress rising until it’s too late; tools feel clinical; forgets to take short breaks.
- How HeartBuddy Helps: Friendly pet that “worries” when stress goes up; prompts a minute of guided breathing; shows streaks for motivation.
- Technical Comfort: Comfortable with web apps and basic settings; no hardware setup preferred.
